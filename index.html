About Us

PAGMehandi designers was founded by Mahalaskhmi from Chennai. Being a traditional Indian women, she was naturally inclined towards the art of mehandi ever since her childhood.

Her passion for the art soon became her profession and she had been practicing this art of mehandi professionally in various organisations for nearly 12 years before founding PAGMehandi designers.

During her professional career, she has done mehandi for various occassions such as weddings, sangeet parties, anniversary parties, birthday parties, dance functions, school functions and just about any Indian festival.

She has served many clients from various parts of Chennai and has always advocated the use of safe methods and makes it a point to use only organic methods in all of her work which can be found in the sections below.
